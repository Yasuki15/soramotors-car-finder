About Sora Motors

Sora Motors is a car dealership based in Victoria, helping customers source quality vehicles from Japan through a smooth and reliable buying process.

Support From Japan

Our head office, operating under a different company name, is located near Tokyo in Japan. If the vehicle can be purchased near Tokyo, we can arrange for one of our staff to inspect it on your behalf.

Preparation Before Shipping

We have our own yard in Japan with our own qualified mechanics and professional panel beaters. We can prepare your vehicle before shipping to Australia, including servicing, wheel changes, parts replacement, scratch repairs, and other requested work.

Registration And Delivery

If you are located in Victoria, we can register the car for you so all you need to do is pick it up. If you are outside Victoria, we can send the car directly to you and provide easy step-by-step guidance on how to register it in your state.
